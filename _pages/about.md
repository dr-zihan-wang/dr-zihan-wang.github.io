I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Science Policy Research Unit (SPRU), University of Sussex Business School. I completed my PhD at the School of Management, University of Bath in July 2025.

My research examines skills as a core regional capability shaping innovation, productivity, labour market dynamics, and structural transformation. Using large-scale quantitative data including online job postings, LinkedIn data, and questionnaires, I investigate how skills are developed, deployed, and transformed across places. With a sectoral focus on manufacturing and the creative industries, I aim to bridge theoretical analysis and policy development, particularly in informing place-based skills policy and industrial strategy.

I am particularly interested in how different types of skills can be developed beyond formal education and training systems, and how they can be leveraged to support regional innovation and industrial transformation, particularly in left-behind places and old industrial regions, through the emergence of new industrial specialisations.

Happy to connect, exchange ideas, and discuss related topics. Consultation and collaboration opportunities are always welcome.
